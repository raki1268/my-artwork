Tokyo i love


Whether it's sunny or rainy, day or night, I love Tokyo. Strictly speaking, I love all the flowers, trees, buildings, sky and land in Tokyo, as well as the people who thrive on this land. A few times I cycled west across Tokyo from Ueno to Shinjuku, then south past Tokyo Tower and then home. I was so tired that I think next time I will take the train, which is not bad either!

Although I just lived and studied in Tokyo for almost a year, I have carefully recorded every happy day! I rode my bicycle around, using my camera and iPhone to record the scenes I wanted to keep, from my apartment home to places near my home and even further afield. This photo album was all taken with an iPhone.

Thanks to Hana who has been with me in Tokyo, as well as my classmates, teachers, and colleagues at work. Because of you, I have always felt the warmth and happiness in this city.

I hope my photos can make people feel cherished, united, loved and peaceful.
