Artist Statement

I am a visual artist and photographer based in Canada and Japan. My work explores the delicate moments of everyday life, capturing the quiet beauty found in urban landscapes, seasonal transitions, and intimate human connections. Through photography and painting, I seek to preserve fleeting emotions and celebrate the simple joys that make life meaningful. My artistic practice is rooted in observation, authenticity, and a deep appreciation for the world around me.
